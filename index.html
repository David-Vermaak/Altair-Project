Airline Seat Optimization




Using Altair Inspire for Topological Optimization of an Aircraft Seat

Project Overview

This senior design project focuses on optimizing the weight of airline seats using Altair Inspire, a powerful topology optimization software. The goal is to reduce the overall weight of aircraft seating while maintaining safety standards and passenger comfort.

Key Objectives
  • Weight reduction
  • Structural integrity
  • Safety compliance
  • Cost efficiency

Methodology

1. Initial Analysis

Detailed analysis of current seat design and identification of optimization opportunities.

2. Altair Inspire

Utilization of Altair Inspire for topology optimization and structural analysis.

3. Validation

Testing and validation of optimized designs to ensure safety and performance.

Technical Specifications

Design Parameters
  • Seat Dimensions: L: 42' W: 20' H: 25'
  • Material: T6061-T6 Aluminum
  • Weight Target: 21 kg
  • Safety Requirements:
    • Direction Loading (G)
    Up 5.4
    Down 9.0
    Forward 9.0
    Back 1.8
    Side 4.0
Analysis Parameters
  • Optimization Goals: Weight reduction by 10%
  • Safety Factors: Factor of Safety of 1.5 for all load cases
  • Manufacturable by: Casting, Machining, or Water Jet Cutting
  • Load Cases:
    • Direction Force (N)
    Up 4131
    Down 6886
    Forward 6886
    Back 1377
    Side 3060
