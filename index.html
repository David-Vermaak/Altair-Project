Airline Seat Optimization

Using Altair Inspire for Weight Optimization in Aircraft Seating

Project Overview

This senior design project focuses on optimizing the weight of airline seats using Altair Inspire, a powerful structural optimization software. The goal is to reduce the overall weight of aircraft seating while maintaining safety standards and passenger comfort.

Key Objectives
  • Weight reduction
  • Structural integrity
  • Safety compliance
  • Cost efficiency

Methodology

1. Initial Analysis

Detailed analysis of current seat design and identification of optimization opportunities.

2. Altair Inspire

Utilization of Altair Inspire for topology optimization and structural analysis.

3. Validation

Testing and validation of optimized designs to ensure safety and performance.
